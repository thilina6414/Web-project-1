CAMPING








Camping is an elective outdoor recreational activity. Generally held, participants leave developed areas to spend time outdoors in more natural
ones in pursuit of activities providing them enjoyment. To be regarded as "camping" a minimum of one night is spent outdoors, distinguishing it
from day-tripping, picnicking, and other similarly short-term recreational activities. Camping can be enjoyed through all four seasons.

One of the more obvious advantages of being on a camping safari is being able to immerse yourself in a 24-hour cycle of eating, sleeping, showering,
walking around (campsite only!) within the park itself. Campers can be a part of the entire natural and wild experience. Having facilities at camp to
fall back on – whether for relaxation, freshening up or having a meal – makes it all more enjoyable so that rather than always administering to that
pressing urge to chase after nature you have the time and the scope to let it come to you. Note: You can camp at all the National Parks in Srilanka.
