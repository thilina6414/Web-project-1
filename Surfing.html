SURFING









The official peak surf season in the south-west, around Weligama, is from the end of October to early April. Winds are offshore
making clean waves, swells are consistently good and rain is at a minimum. The season then shifts up the south east coast towards Arugum Bay
from April. Outside the official surfing season you still get some fantastic days in the water. For beginners Weligama is perfect all year round
for learning to surf.

Places you may visit:
Southwest coast- Hikkaduwa / Narigama
South Coast- Galle/ Tangalle/ Kablana/ Midigama/ Waligama/ Mirissa / Matara/ Dikwella
Southeast Coast: Arugambay
