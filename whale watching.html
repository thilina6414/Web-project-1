WHALE WATCHING









Whale and Dolphin watching is an easy sport that you can take up during the months of December to April. All you need to take is a boat,
few kilometers from the shore, which we can help you arrange. The ever smiling, mischievous dolphins will put on a show while some of the oldest
and the largest sea creatures, the humpback and the blue whales, will nonchalantly glide past you, when you embark on a whale/ dolphin watching
excursion off the Southern, Eastern or the West coast of Sri Lanka.
Places you may visit:
Mirissa
Trincomalee
Kalpitiya
